PDpsix

Computes psi function (derivative of rho function) times x for minimum density power divergence estimator

More About

Function PDpsix transforms vector u as follows \[ PDpsix(u,alpha)= \alpha u^2 \exp(-\alpha (u^2/2)); \]

References

Riani, M. Atkinson, A.C., Corbellini A. and Perrotta A. (2020), Robust Regression with Density Power Divergence: Theory, Comparisons and Data Analysis, submitted.
