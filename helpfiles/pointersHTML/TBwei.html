TBwei

Computes weight function psi(u)/u for Tukey's biweight

More About

function TBrho transforms vector u as follows \[ TBrho(u)= \left\{ \begin{array}{cc} (c^2/6) \left\{ 1-[1-(u/c)^2]^3 \right\} & |u/c| \leq 1 \\ (c^2/6) & |u/c| >1 \\ \end{array} \right. \] See equation (2.37) p. 29 of Maronna et al. (2006). Remark: equation (2.37) is written in standardized terms in such a way that $\rho(c)=1$, so it is the previous expression divided by $(c^2/6)$

References

Maronna, R.A., Martin D. and Yohai V.J. (2006), "Robust Statistics, Theory and Methods", Wiley, New York.
