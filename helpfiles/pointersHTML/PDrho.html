PDrho

Computes rho function for minimum density power divergence estimator

More About

function PDrho transforms vector u as follows \[ PDrho(u,alpha)= 1-\exp(-\alpha (u^2/2)); \]

References

Riani, M. Atkinson, A.C., Corbellini A. and Perrotta A. (2020), Robust Regression with Density Power Divergence: Theory, Comparisons and Data Analysis, Entropy, Vol. 22, 399. https://www.mdpi.com/1099-4300/22/4/399
