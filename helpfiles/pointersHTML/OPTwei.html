OPTwei

OPTwei Computes weight function psi(u)/u for optimal weight function

Description

More About

Function OPTwei transforms vector u as follows \[ OPTwei(u,c) = \left\{ \begin{array}{cc} 1/(3.25*c^2) & |u| \leq 2 \\ (1/3.25) \left( -1.944 * 1 / c^2 + 1.728 \frac{u^2}{c^4} - 0.312\frac{u^2}{c^6} + 0.016 \frac{u.^6}{c^8} \right) & \qquad 2c\leq |u|\leq 3c \\ 0 \end{array} \right. \] Remark: Yohai and Zamar (1997) showed that the optimal $\rho$ function is optimal in the following highly desirable sense: the final M estimate has a breakdown point of one-half and minimizes the maximum bias under contamination distributions (locally for small fraction of contamination), subject to achieving a desidered nominal asymptotic efficiency when the data are Gaussian.
