HUrho

HUrho Computes rho function for Huber

Description

More About

function HUrho transforms vector u as follows \[ HUrho(u)= \left\{ \begin{array}{cc} (u^2/2) & |u/c| \leq 1 \\ c|u| -c^2/2 & |u/c| >1 \\ \end{array} \right. \] See equation (2.27) p. 26 of Maronna et al. (2006). Remark: equation (2.26) is written in standardized terms in such a way that $\rho(c)=1$, so it is the previous expression multiplied by $2$

References

Maronna, R.A., Martin D. and Yohai V.J. (2006), Robust Statistics, Theory and Methods, Wiley, New York.
