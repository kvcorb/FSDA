The name comes from the fact that among all ellipsoids {x: d(x,µ, Ʃ)} containing at least half of the data points, the one given by the MVE estimate has minimum volume (i.e. minimum |Ʃ|).

The MVE estimate of location and  of the covariance matrix  can be found using routine mve.
