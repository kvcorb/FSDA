ASrho

Computes rho function for Andrew's sine function

More About

function ASrho transforms vector u as follows \[ ASrho(u)= \left\{ \begin{array}{cc} c (1-\cos (u / c)) & |u/c| \leq \pi \\ 2c & |u/c| > \pi \\ \end{array} \right. \]
